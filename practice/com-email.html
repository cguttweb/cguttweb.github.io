The Carnival of Madness is a hard rock/alternative rock tour that first began in 2010 in the US. It is a number of rock bands performing together on one night of great rock music. It has made its way across the Atlantic to debut on UK shores in 2016. This year the bands performing are Black Stone Cherry as headliners with support from Halestorm and Shinedown.

Rockers Black Stone Cherry are from Edmonton, Kentucky and were founded in 2001. The band members are Chris Robertson (lead vocals, lead guitar), Jon Lawhon (bass guitar, backing vocals), Ben Wells (rhythm guitar, backing vocals) and John Fred Young (drums, backing vocals). Their latest album Kentucky was released in April 2016. Halestorm began life in 2007 and originate from Red Lion, Pennsylvania; although their first self titled studio album was not released until 2009. The current band members are Lzzy Hale (lead vocals, guitar), her brother Arejay Hale (drums, percussion), Joe Hottinger (guitar) and Josh Smith (bass). Shinedown formed in 2001 and hail from Jacksonville in Florida. The current band members are Brent Smith (vocals), Zach Myers (guitar), Eric Bass and Barry Kerch (drums). They have released five studio albums to date with the latest being Threat to Survival in 2015.
